Allan Johnson

Allan Johnson is a renowned author, speaker, and sociologist. With a passion for exploring social justice and inequality, Allan has made significant contributions through his writings and public speeches. This blog captures some of the highlights of his work and thoughts.

The Impact of Social Structures

Allan Johnson explores how social structures influence individual behavior and contribute to systemic inequalities. Through his insightful analysis, he sheds light on the importance of understanding and challenging these structures to create a more just society.

Confronting Privilege

In this article, Allan discusses the concept of privilege and its pervasive impact on society. He encourages readers to confront their own privileges and use their positions to advocate for equality and justice for all.
